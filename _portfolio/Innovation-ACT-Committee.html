It's that time of year again! Canberras largest entrepreneural program Innovation ACT (iACT) brings together likeminded idea driven entrepreneurs from around Canberra every year.

This year I am lucky to be able to join a committee of iACT alumnis to help budding businesses going through the iACT program

The alumni committee attends the business development workshops as mentors to give advice and aid entreprenurs as they grow there idea/startup throughout the program
