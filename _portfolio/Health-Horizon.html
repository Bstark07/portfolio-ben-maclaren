"We often get glimpses of incredible advances in health on the news, but too often never hear about them again," - Marcus Dawe, CEO of Health Horizon.

I've been working for the past few months with Health Horizons a health innovation company that idenitfies catalogues and tracks health innovations around the world. This allows people, medical practicioners and hospitals to follow and keep up to date with health innovations that are important to them.

1. Article: Three Games Changing How We See Autism

My first piece of work was a article for their medium blog, after browsing through the health horizons database I settled on three games that help raise awareness for autism.

This is a topic close to me, most members of my family and alot of my friends are autistic, there are alot of different struggles they all face, hopefully these games will help others connect with them and aid in understanding others whose experiences are different from our own.

You can read my medium article here: Three Games Changing How We See Autism!

4. Data Analysis

Health Horizon maintains a database of over 800 Health innovations but had not done any visualisations of their dataset. Visualising a data set and experimenting with data can allow a business especially in the data industry to gain insights into different potential avenues of communications and perspectives.

This stage mostly involved cleaning data that was kindly provided to menu by HH, unfortunately due to security and privacy I can't share any of the data on here
